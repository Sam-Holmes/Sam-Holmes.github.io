BOIDS


Client: University
Role: Development & Design

About

Following Craig Reynolds's priciples created basic flocking AI in 3D space.
This project was my first time ever working with Artificial Intelligence, and as such there was a lot I didn't know going in. Though the project was simple and the rules were already known, it was very interesting seeing some of the immergence and the 'life' that my system created.
During the project I was exposed to a lot of the key AI principles, besides the three laws, that greatly improved my knowledge and understanding of AI. This also piqued my interest in AI and as a result I began to analyse the games I played trying to work out how their AI functioned.
As the project was set in 2D space I wanted to make my work stand out by making the project run via 3D space. Though this wasn't a enormous task I feel it produced nice results that worked to make my project stand out.
I would love to go back and expand the behaviours of the boids, adding things like hunger, breeding and possibly evolution. This would require giving each boid a set of stats that could affect the way it behaves/moves. Once that has been achieved I can then extend that to consumption of other boids or other items effect the stats in different ways, leading to a very simplified version of evolution.
